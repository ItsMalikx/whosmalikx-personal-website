Privacy Policy

Revision Date: March 29, 2026

Overview

This website is operated by an individual (“Operator”), Malik, located in Texas, United States. The website consists of the primary domain whosmalikx.com and its associated subdomains, including lcna.whosmalikx.com, which serves as a Limbus Company news archive.

This Privacy Policy sets forth the principles governing how information is handled in connection with the use of the website and its services.

General Principle

The website is designed to minimize the collection and processing of data.

No personal information is intentionally collected, stored, or sold.

User Activity

Any network activity occurs solely as a direct result of user-initiated actions, including but not limited to:

  • Accessing pages
  • Loading content
  • Navigating between domains or subdomains

The website does not engage in background data collection beyond what is necessary to deliver requested content.

Local Data

The Operator does not store personal data on servers.

Any data stored locally (such as browser cache) remains under the user’s control and may be removed at any time through browser settings.

Technical Processing

Limited, non-identifiable technical data may be processed by infrastructure providers for the purposes of:

  • Delivering content
  • Maintaining performance
  • Ensuring security
  • Preventing abuse

Such data is not used to identify individuals and is not retained longer than necessary.

Analytics

The website does not employ invasive tracking mechanisms or collect personally identifiable analytics data.

To the extent analytics are used, they are:

  • Fully anonymized
  • Non-invasive
  • Not used to track individual users

No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics processes.

Infrastructure

The website is deployed and delivered through third-party infrastructure, specifically:

  • Cloudflare (hosting, CDN, DNS, and security services)
  • GitHub (repository hosting, development, and related site infrastructure services)

These providers may process limited technical information, such as IP addresses and request metadata, as part of standard operation.

Such processing is governed by the respective provider’s own privacy policies.

No additional third-party services (including advertising networks or payment processors) are used unless explicitly stated.

Scope

This Privacy Policy applies exclusively to:

  • whosmalikx.com
  • All associated subdomains, including lcna.whosmalikx.com

This Policy does not apply to:

  • External websites linked from this site
  • Third-party services not operated or controlled by the Operator

Data Rights

Because no personal data is intentionally collected, typical data rights (including access, correction, or deletion requests) may not apply.

If you believe personal data has been processed in error, you may contact:

malikx.biz@gmail.com

Requests will be reviewed and addressed in accordance with applicable law.

Children

The website is not directed toward individuals under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by applicable jurisdiction).

No personal data is knowingly collected from children.

Security

Reasonable safeguards are implemented, including:

  • HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Cloudflare security protections

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.

Changes

Any revisions will be reflected by updating the revision date above. Continued use of the website constitutes acceptance of any changes.
